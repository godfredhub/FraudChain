Stay Ahead of Scammers

FraudChain gives you actionable advice to help secure your wallets, protect your identity, and avoid digital traps in the blockchain world.

Double-Check Links

Always verify URLs before clicking. Phishing sites mimic real platforms but steal your keys and tokens.

Don’t Share Private Keys

No trusted source will ask for your seed phrase or private key. Keep them offline and secure.

Avoid Unknown Airdrops

Free token drops can sometimes be traps—double-check the project before connecting your wallet.

Use Hardware Wallets

Protect large amounts of crypto with a hardware wallet instead of browser-based extensions.

DYOR (Do Your Own Research)

Research projects and team credibility. Don’t rely solely on hype, shills, or influencers.

Check Scam Reports

Use FraudChain to search for addresses or contacts reported for fraud before interacting.
